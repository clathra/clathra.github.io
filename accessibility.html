Accessibility

Accessibility statement

Last updated: 19 June 2026

We want clathra.com to be usable by as many people as possible, including those who rely on assistive technology. We aim to meet the WCAG 2.1 Level AA standard.

What we have done

  • Semantic, structured HTML with a logical heading order and page landmarks.
  • Full keyboard navigation, a skip link, and visible focus outlines.
  • Text alternatives for meaningful images, and decorative animation hidden from assistive technology.
  • Colour contrast checked against the AA threshold for text and controls.
  • Labelled form fields, and respect for the reduced-motion setting.

Known limitations

We rely on a third-party form provider for the contact form, so parts of its behaviour are outside our direct control. The animated background is purely decorative and is hidden from screen readers. We treat accessibility as ongoing work and review the site as it changes.

Telling us about a problem

If you run into a barrier on this site, or need information in a different format, please email contact@clathra.com. Describe the page and the problem, and we will respond and put it right as quickly as we can.
